What to Expect

An honest, friendly look at what happens during a Sunday service at Eden Life.

No Surprises Here

A Sunday at Eden Life, From Start to Finish

Here's a straightforward picture of what happens when you join us on a Sunday.

🎵

Worship

We open every service with 20–30 minutes of music — a mix of contemporary praise and heartfelt worship. You don't need to know the songs. Just come ready to encounter God.

📖

The Word

Every week, [Add pastor name] or one of our teaching team delivers a practical, Bible-centred message. Sermons are relevant, clear, and grounded in Scripture.

🙏

Prayer

Prayer is central to Eden Life. Every service includes time for corporate prayer — and prayer ministers are available after the service for personal prayer.

🤝

Community

After the service, we stay, we chat, we connect. There is no rush to leave. This is a community, not just a crowd.

⏱️

Duration

Services typically run [Add duration — e.g. 90 minutes]. There is no pressure to stay for the entire service if it's your first time, but most people do — and stay longer after.

💛

The Offering

An offering is taken during the service. As a guest, you are never expected or pressured to give. Giving is a personal act of worship for those who choose to participate.

Join Us This Sunday

We are better in person. Come and experience Eden Life for yourself.
