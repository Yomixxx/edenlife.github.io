SNOW

Supernatural Night of Wonders — a night set apart for encounter, intercession, and the miraculous.

Annual Faith Gathering

What Is SNOW?

SNOW — Supernatural Night of Wonders — is Eden Life's flagship annual gathering. It is a night set apart entirely for extended worship, powerful proclamation of the Word, corporate intercession, and the expectation of God's supernatural intervention.

Hundreds gather from across Lagos and beyond — believers who come hungry, carrying personal needs, family burdens, and corporate prayers. What makes SNOW different from a regular service is the atmosphere of focused, sustained faith that builds through the night.

What Happens at SNOW

  • Extended corporate worship and praise
  • Proclamation of the Word
  • Corporate intercession and targeted prayer
  • Testimonies from the community
  • Prayer for healing, breakthrough, and specific needs
  • Commissioning and declaration for the year ahead
