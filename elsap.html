Eden Life Skill Acquisition Program — practical skills training that transforms livelihoods and communities.

Skills & Community Development

What Is El-SAP?

El-SAP — the Eden Life Skill Acquisition Program — is one of our most direct expressions of the mission to provide "relevant empowerment skills, tools, and platforms for the improvement of human life, society advancement, and nation building."

El-SAP provides structured vocational and professional skills training to community members in Lagos, with a particular focus on young people, the unemployed, and the underemployed. Participants learn skills they can immediately apply — whether as employees, entrepreneurs, or community contributors.
