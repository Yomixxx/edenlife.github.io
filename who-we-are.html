Mission

"We Equip People for Christ and a Life of Exploits. We are sent to Teach, Preach and Minister the Person, Principles and the Power of Christ — and to provide relevant empowerment skills, tools and platforms for the improvement of human life, society advancement and nation building."

What We Believe

Core Beliefs

We believe the Bible is the inspired, authoritative Word of God — the foundation of every Eden Life teaching and programme.
We believe Jesus is the Son of God, crucified for our sins, risen from the dead, and seated at the Father's right hand. He is the centre of our message.
Salvation is by grace through faith in Jesus alone. Every person can be forgiven, transformed, and made new through a personal relationship with Christ.
The Holy Spirit lives in every believer, empowering them for holiness, fruitfulness, and effective witness. We welcome His gifts and guidance.
The local church is God's primary instrument for discipleship and social transformation. Eden Life exists to be that church — relevant, rooted, and outward-looking.
Every human being bears the image of God. This conviction drives our empowerment programmes and our care for the communities we serve.
