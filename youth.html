Where Young People Belong

Eden Pulse is the youth community of Eden Life Experience Centre — a vibrant, authentic space for teenagers and young adults (ages [Add range]) to encounter Christ, build genuine friendships, and discover who they are called to be.

What Eden Pulse Offers

More Than a Youth Group

⚡

Vibrant Worship

Youth-led worship that is real, energetic, and deeply sincere. A space to encounter God without pretence.

📖

Relevant Teaching

Bible-based teaching that addresses the actual questions and pressures young people face today — identity, purpose, relationships, social media, and more.

👥

Community & Friendship

Small groups, hangouts, and shared experiences that build the kind of friendships that last a lifetime.

🌍

Outreach & Service

Opportunities to serve the community, participate in mission, and make a tangible difference as young people.

🎤

Leadership Development

Eden Pulse intentionally develops young leaders — giving them real responsibility and real platforms to grow.

🎉

Events & Experiences

Retreats, camps, conferences, and special events throughout the year that create shared memories and spiritual breakthroughs.
