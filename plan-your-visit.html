🚌 Getting Here

Ogudu Road is accessible by bus and ride-hailing services. If you are on the Island, we offer a free shuttle — contact us to arrange it.

👗 Dress Code

Smart casual. Come as you are — what matters is that you come.

When You Arrive

1

Find the Welcome Desk

Our welcome team will be at the entrance to greet you, answer questions, and help you find a seat.

2

Drop Off the Kids

Head to the Children's check-in area before the service starts — our team will welcome your children and explain the programme.

3

Enjoy the Service

Relax. Worship. Listen. You're not expected to know the songs or the routines — just be present.

4

Stay for Coffee

After the service, our welcome team will find you for a quick chat. We'd love to meet you.

Have Questions Before You Come?

Our team is happy to answer any questions about what to expect, how to find us, or anything else on your mind.
