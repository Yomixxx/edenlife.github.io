Give to Eden Life

Every gift directly funds programs that equip people for Christ and a life of purpose, skill, and community impact.

Why Give

Fuel the Mission

Eden Life is a faith-based ministry sustained by the generous giving of those who believe in the power of the Gospel to transform lives — spiritually, practically, and socially. Your gift directly funds:

  • El-SAP skills training for unemployed and underemployed community members
  • EQUIP discipleship resources and facilitation
  • SNOW, Activate, Soar, and God Moment event costs
  • Mainland and Island campus operations
  • Online church production and media ministry
  • Community outreach and support initiatives
